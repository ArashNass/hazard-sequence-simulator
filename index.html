Welcome

One event rarely tells the whole story.

This simulator explores how earthquakes, aftershocks, heavy rain and landslides can occur while an asset is still recovering. Choose a scenario, keep or change the hazard toggles, then run one reproducible sequence plus a paired Monte Carlo comparison.

What does “disruption” mean here?The disruption index is a dimensionless screening scale from 0 to 100%. It represents relative operational impairment in this model—not physical damage, financial loss, downtime probability or a code-based performance level.

The event laws are transparent, but the interaction and consequence relationships are deliberately simplified. Results are educational scenario indicators, not site-specific hazard, design, insurance or emergency outputs.
